Monkey C Juice

Description

Snack time, any time's the right time for this delicious banana and orange juice drink. This easy smoothie recipe is a 5-star favorite, and it's ready in less than 5 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 Banana
  • 8 oz orange juice
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Place juice and banana in blender. Blend on high until smooth and frothy. about 30 seconds.
  2. Pour mixture into your favorite cup and enjoy!!
