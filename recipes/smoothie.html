Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

Description

Breakfast, lunch, dessert, or snack time, any time's the right time for this delicious banana smoothie. With hundreds of ratings and reviews from our Allrecipes community, this easy smoothie recipe is a 5-star favorite, and it's ready in less than 5 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Banana
  • Milk
  • Peanut butter
  • Honey
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. chop 2 bananas in to bits sizes and throw in blender
  2. pour 2 cups of your favorite type of milk in the blender
  3. add 2 heep table spoon of peanut butter to the blender
  4. add 1 table spoon of honey to the blender
  5. Finally add 1 cup of ice to the blender and blend for 2 minutes at 3o second intervals
  6. Pour mixture into your favorite cup and enjoy!!
